Our Story

A Home Away From Home in the Mountains

Situated just 50-55 km from Kutaisi and close to the E60 highway, Ubisa Family Guesthouse is your perfect stopover on a Georgian road trip or a dedicated escape from city life.

We offer a unique opportunity to experience authentic local life. My family prepares delicious dishes from Georgian cuisine, including our famous Khachapuri and homemade wine. Whether you want to hike, fish, or simply relax on the terrace, our doors are open.
